🌾 Garden Defense

Keep your farm alive as long as you can, farmer!

Controls

W A S D Move around the farm
Mouse Look around
Click Use selected tool / interact
1-6 Select toolbar item
E Harvest / interact with crop
B Open/close shop
Esc Deselect tool

How to Play

🌱 Plant seeds on soil patches → crops grow over time
🌿 Towers auto-attack enemies in range
🧂 Salt kills slugs & snails on click
🌾 Harvest crops → sell at market for 💰
⚠️ Droughts and tornadoes damage your farm
💀 Game over when you have no crops AND no money
